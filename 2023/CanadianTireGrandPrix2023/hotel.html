     Located just a one-minute walk from the subway in the centre of downtown, Montreal Marriott Chateau Champlain sits beside the multifunctional Bell Centre, host to concerts, circus performances and hockey games. Wander the cobblestone streets and spirited plazas of Old Montreal before taking in the city’s iconic Clock Tower. Nearby Rue Saint-Paul is home to charming shops and eateries, while Montreal Biodome recreates vast tropical and subpolar ecosystems. Upon your return to our modern hotel, pair cocktails with bistro-style urban cuisine at our restaurant and bar.
