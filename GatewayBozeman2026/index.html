Welcome to Bozeman and Big Sky Country

Nestled in the Gallatin Valley at the foot of the Bridger Mountains, Bozeman is Montana’s gateway to adventure. It’s an experience that captures the wild spirit of the American West. Near the entrance to Yellowstone National Park, Bozeman offers adventures, breathtaking vista and a community that feels like home. Whether you’re snowmobiling, skiing, hiking, fishing or simply stargazing, Bozeman is the perfect place to be.

Bozeman offers the ultimate launchpad for an exhilarating snowmobiling adventure into the winter wonderland of Yellowstone National Park. Its blend of world-class trails, and breathtaking backcountry access make it an ideal base for exploring Yellowstone’s wonders, wildlife & landscapes.

If skiing is your passion, the nearby Big Sky Resort boasts over 5,800 skiable acres and an average of 400 inches of snow per year earning its reputation as “Biggest Skiing in America.” With 36 lifts, skiers and snowboarders alike can spread out across uncrowded slopes and take in the majesty of this special place. At the summit of Lone Peak, expert skiers can enjoy the panoramic views of three states, before their heart-pounding descent from over 11,000 feet. But rest assured that whatever level you are at, Big Sky will be a phenomenal place to experience the beauty and spirit that Montana evokes.

While in Bozeman you will call the Kimpton Armory Hotel home. The Armory is the city’s first and only 4-star luxury boutique retreat, blending historic charm with modern indulgence. For discriminating traveler wishing to see Bozeman at its finest, the Kimpton Armory Hotel is the only choice.
