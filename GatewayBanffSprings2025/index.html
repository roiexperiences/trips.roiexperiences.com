Nestled in the heart of the Canadian Rockies in Alberta, Banff is one of Canada’s most breathtaking resort towns. It is surrounded by towering snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear turquoise lakes and vast evergreen forests. The town lies within Banff National Park making it a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Summertime in Banff attracts adventurers with its world-class hiking, mountain biking and rock climbing, while nearby Lake Louise and Moraine Lake provide postcard-perfect settings for kayaking, canoeing or simply taking in the views. Wildlife sightings – elk, bears, bighorn sheep – are common, adding a thrilling edge to the experience.
