For guests seeking a sanctuary for the senses, Secrets Spa by Pevonia at Secrets Puerto Los Cabos offers a sublime atmosphere of pure indulgence. Pamper yourself in our stunning spa surrounded by a hydrotherapy circuit, sauna and steam room. A Swiss shower, sauna, steam room, private shower and a relaxing indoor lounge area are here for you to enjoy. Massages, body treatments, facials, manicures, pedicures and a full service beauty salon are available for you. The spa also offers an array of rejuvenating facials, spa packages and pampering for your hands, feet and nails.

Hours: 8 a.m. - 8 p.m.

  • 13 indoor treatments rooms (Two of them are Master Cabins, for couples treatments)
