Secrets Akumal Riviera Maya, ideally located in the heart of the Riviera Maya along the famed white-sand beaches and calm, clear waters of Akumal, fuses elegant and original design with nature's elements. Rated as one of the "Top 10 Beaches in Mexico" by TripAdvisor and with a name meaning "place of the turtle" in Mayan, Akumal is renowned for its beautiful beaches and green sea turtle population making this oasis a premium snorkeling location. The world's second largest barrier reef sits just 150 meters off the resort's beach. This stunning new resort offers adults a charming, picturesque escape complete with an exceptional Unlimited-Luxury experience.
