Welcome to the Mayan Riviera, boasting world-class beaches and warm turquoise waters. Playa del Carmen, originally a fishing village, has retained its village charm and artists colony while blending with a European influence to offer hip night spots, trendy cafes and colorful shops. The island of Cozumel is a short ferry ride away.

This resort is a beautiful combination of luxury and exquisite architecture showcasing the splendor of the Yucatan peninsula. Secrets Moxché is located just a 10-minute drive from the heart of Playa del Carmen and 5th Avenue. A quick 35 minutes from the Cancun International Airport, Secrets Moxché offers breathtaking views in an upscale setting with earthy undertones.

The 485 suites feature spacious accommodations with ocean and tropical views and include private swim out options. Guests will experience the stylish, vibrant locale with the extensive inclusions of UnlimitedLuxury® and enjoy the privileges of 24-hour room and concierge service, a mini bar in each room, WIFI, a soaking tub, among many other convenient features. This resort offers 11 gourmet dining options,including 7 a la carte restaurants (with two located directly on the beach), a buffet, grill, a café, and bars and lounges.

* Secrets is an Adults only resort.
