Mina's Fish House
James Beard Award-winning Chef Michael Mina brings a line-to-table philosophy to our take on the traditional Hawaiian fish house, where friends and family gather for beachfront meals paired with local brews, tropical cocktails and panoramic ocean views at our unique seafood restaurant in Oahu.

La Hiki
Located alongside the Ohana Pool, La Hiki offers open-air dining in the heart of the Resort. Inspired by the Island's bounty of rich and diverse local farms, La Hiki's plant-forward menu is rooted in fresh, sustainable cuisine.

Noe
Celebrates time-honoured Southern Italian cuisine with a modern attitude. Led by Michelin-starred, Japanese-Italian chef Ryo Takatsuka, this famed Italian restaurant in Oahu is a remix of traditional Southern Italian ingredients and age-old recipes, impeccably prepared and presented in a reimagined contemporary outdoor setting.

Hokulea
Our island-inspired, open-air coffee bar is the perfect place to start the day with an expertly blended coffee. Hokulea specializes in locally crafted, artisanal coffee, as well as fresh pastries, baked goods, organic salads and sandwiches.

Manalo Lounge
Inspired by the Hawaiian word for "sweet water" - when Oahu's fresh mountain water merges harmoniously with the salty ocean = Manalo Lounge celebrates Hawaii's diverse mix of cultures that have influenced the area's cuisine. Gather with family and friends for dinner in a contemporary yet casual setting at the water's edge.
