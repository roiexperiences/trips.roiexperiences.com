Prepare to be pampered on board! Avalon prides itself on their chef-prepared and regionally-inspired meals. Beyond, regional and local cuisines, meal options also include classical favorites as well as selections from the Avalon Fresh menu which is composed of healthier, special options to maintain every diet requirement. Your voyage includes all meals once aboard. Meals are taken in the spacious open-seating of the Panorama Dining Room, but of course room service is always an option.
  • There is a breakfast buffet every morning with all the favorites you expect, in addition to daily a la carte specials.
  • For lunch, you may enjoy the buffet and daily specials of the Panorama Dining Room or enjoy dining outdoors at the Sky Grill (located on the Sky Deck), weather permitting. If something lighter is more your style, enjoy a light snack in the Lounge.
  • Afternoon Tea is featured daily with tea, coffee & sweet treats
  • Dinner is a four-course selected menu in The Dining Room with your choice of appetizers, salads, soups, entrees, desserts or cheese.
  • There is also a Bistro Tasting menu for a less formal dining experience
  • Complimentary wine, beer & soft drinks are offered with lunch and dinner
  • No set seating times or tables
  • Each meal offers vegetarian, vegan, low calorie, low fat, high fiber, low cholesterol, gluten-free & allergy-free options. Your server can guide you toward the appropriate selections
  • Panorama Lounge offers premium spirits, panoramic windows, cozy seating and a dance floor so party can continue
