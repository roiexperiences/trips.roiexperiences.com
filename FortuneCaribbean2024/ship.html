The Allure of the Seas is almost four football fields long at 1,187 feet from bow to stern and 215 feet wide. On the Allure of the Seas discover what all 18 exciting, fun-filled decks have to offer. Discover the variety of dining options, shopping experiences, jam-packed activity offerings, assortment of pools, and a multitude of bars to enjoy your getaway. If excitement or relaxation is what you are seeking, Allure of the Seas is sure to offer something for everyone.
  • Dance the night away in one of the night clubs or lounges.
  • Enjoy the solitude of the adults-only Solarium pool.
  • Be mesmerized by one of the amazing entertainment shows.
  • Test your luck at the Casino Royale.
  • Feel the adrenaline rush and soar ten decks above the BoardWalk on the complimentary zipline.
  • Ride some waves with the signature 40-foot-long surf simulator, Flow Rider.
  • Climb the 40-foot ascent and view the vast ocean from the Rock Climbing Wall.
  • Be mesmerized at the AquaTheater show, featuring deck-defying stunts.
  • Take a stroll through Central Park, filled with 20,000 plants and cozy corner cafes.
  • Shop until your heart is content at the Royal Promenade.
  • Go for a carousel ride on the BoardWalk.
  • If you have kids in tow, check out the Youth Zone and Adventure Ocean for ultimate fun.
  • H2O zone is perfect for your littlest family members, where they can play in the water themed activities.
