Royal Caribbean's Allure of the Seas delivers exhilarating onboard experiences, award-winning entertainment. You find seven distinct neighborhoods, 25 dining options, and 2,384 crew waiting to serve you. The Allure of the Seas provides a never-ending supply of things to do with many entertainment venues, restaurants, bars, and pools to occupy your time. As a member of the Oasis Class, this beautiful ship shares the same neighborhood concept as her sister ships, being the most popular ones: Central Park, the Boardwalk, the Royal Promenade, and the Entertainment Place. Keep active at sea at the ice skating rink, the fitness center and the rock climbing wall. There's even a zip line for those looking for a bit of a thrill on your cruise. Be sure to also check out FlowRider, Royal Caribbean's hottest spot for simulated surfing. Other entertainment options include culinary classes, Broadway at Sea, outdoor movie nights, bars, an arcade, a library, Adventure Ocean Theater, different varieties of live entertainment and much more.

With everything that Allure of the Seas has to offer you can decide to have days packed with tons of adventures or enjoy having an open calendar and take the days as they come.
