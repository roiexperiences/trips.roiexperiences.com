ACTIVITIES

Cenotes and Paradise Lagoon
Navigate through breathtaking cenotes and caves and marvel at stalacites and stalagmites as you explore the ancient rock formations up close! Then snorkel through an incredible lagoon brimming with aquatic life!

Luxury Sailing & Snorkel
Sail away on a Caribbean catamaran adventure! Kick back in turquoise waves, before gearing up for a splashin' snorkel session. Dive into crystal-clear waters, and swim next to vibrant coral reefs - A not to miss expereince when going to the Caribbean!

Taco Tour
Indulge in Mexico's taco culture on this mouth-watering tour! Visit for "taquerias", trying iconic tacos like Cochinita Pibil and Pastor. Sample classic sides and local beer at each stop, and enjoy dessert at Parque de las Palapas, featuring traditional Churros and Marquesitas. A delightful journey into Mexican flavors and traditions! (Vegetarian options available)
