THE RESORT

Nestled on Mexico's Caribbean coast, Secrets Riviera Cancun Resort & Spa is a luxurious, adults-only paradise where white-sand beaches meet crystal-clear turquoise waters. This all-suite escape offers Preferred Club privileges, personalized service, and Unlimited-Luxury inclusions-perfect for relaxation, romance, and indulgence.

As a guest, you enjoy exclusive free-flow access to the neighboring Breathless Riviera Cancun Resort & Spa, just a few steps away, unlocking the best of both worlds. Spend your mornings in Secrets' serene, upscale atmosphere-lounging by the tranquil Preferred Club pool or savoring gourmet cuisine at Rosewater-then stroll next door for the vibrant energy, live music, and social buzz of Breathless.

With dual-resort access, you can:
  • Enjoy 11 restaurants and multiple bars, all reservation-free and serving top-shelf drinks.
  • Switch between peaceful, private pools and lively, DJ-hosted swim-up parties.
  • Indulge in spa treatments, explore Cancun's beaches, or dive into nightly entertainment.

    It' a seamless blend of tranquility and excitement-where you can unwind in the lap of luxury or turn up the energy whenever you choose.
